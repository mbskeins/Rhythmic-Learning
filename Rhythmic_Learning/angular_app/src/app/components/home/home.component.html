Why Rhythmic Learning?













Problem Statement:



Learning at a young age can sometimes seem daunting, and down right frustrating when intrest isn't there

What we wanted to do with this project was to bridge the gap from education, to young kids

With a lot of research, we decided to leverage one of the biggest resources for information in the world, Wikipedia

We take a Wikipedia page, generate a rap using AI, generate a beat, and play it back for the user












Impact:







Better Grades

More Engagment

Backed By Science
